⚙️ One-time setup

This app stores your notes as files in your own OneDrive. To connect it, it needs an Application (client) ID from a free Microsoft app registration. The setup guide that came with this app (SETUP.md) walks you through getting one in about 10 minutes.

The ID is saved only on this device. You'll enter it once per device.

📝 My Notes

Your personal note-taking app. Notes are stored as portable Markdown files in your OneDrive — accessible from every device, owned by you.
